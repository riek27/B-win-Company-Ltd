Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get a quote for my project?

Fill out our contact form with project details, or call us directly. We'll schedule a site visit (if in Juba) or a virtual consultation to understand your requirements before providing a detailed quote.

What areas do you serve?

We are based in Juba but serve clients across South Sudan. For projects outside Juba, we arrange temporary site offices and manage logistics accordingly.

How long does a typical construction project take?

Project duration depends on size and complexity. A standard residential house takes 4-6 months, while commercial buildings may take 6-12 months. We provide detailed timelines in our project proposals.

Do you provide design services?

Yes, we have in-house architects who can create custom designs or work with your existing plans. We handle everything from concept to completion.

What is your payment structure?

We typically work with milestone-based payments: 30% deposit, 40% during construction, 25% upon completion, and 5% after final inspection. Specific terms are detailed in our contracts.

Do you handle permits and approvals?

Yes, we assist with all necessary permits, approvals, and regulatory compliance as part of our comprehensive construction services.
