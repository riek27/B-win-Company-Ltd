Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical website project take?

Our typical luxury website projects take between 1-2 weeks from discovery to launch. The timeline depends on the complexity of the project and the responsiveness of both teams during the review process.

Do you work with builders outside of your local area?

Yes, we work with luxury home builders nationwide. Our process is designed to work effectively remotely, with regular video calls and collaborative online tools to ensure seamless communication.

What is your pricing structure?

We offer custom pricing based on the scope of each project. Our luxury website projects typically start at $3,000 to $7000+ depend on what your need. We provide detailed proposals after an initial discovery call to understand your specific needs.

Do you provide ongoing support after launch?

Yes, we offer various ongoing support and maintenance packages to ensure your website remains secure, up-to-date, and performing optimally. We also provide training for your team to manage content updates.
